Mr. White

A social deduction game

How to Play

👤

Three Roles

  • Civilians — all get the same secret word
  • Imposter — gets a similar but different word
  • Mr. White — gets no word at all, must bluff
💬

Clues

Everyone says one word out loud as a clue. Civilians hint at their word. The Imposter and Mr. White must blend in!

👍

Vote

Pick a player to eliminate, then accuse them: are they the Imposter or Mr. White?

🏆

Win Conditions

  • Civilians win — eliminate both the Imposter AND Mr. White (who guesses wrong)
  • Mr. White wins — survives to 2 players, or guesses the secret word correctly
  • Imposter wins — survives to 2 players, or Mr. White is caught but guesses wrong while Imposter lives

Mr. White

A social deduction game

How to Play

👤

Roles

All players except Mr. White receive the same secret word. Mr. White gets no word.

💬

Clues

Each player gives exactly one word as a clue related to the secret word. Mr. White must bluff!

👍

Vote

Players discuss and vote to eliminate who they think is Mr. White. Most votes gets eliminated.

🆕

Guess

If Mr. White is eliminated, they get one chance to guess the secret word.

🏆

Win Conditions

  • Civilians win if Mr. White is eliminated and guesses wrong.
  • Mr. White wins if they survive to the final 2 players OR correctly guess the word.
